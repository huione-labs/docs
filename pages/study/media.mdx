## Our Font

Our brand typography system is designed to ensure visual consistency and readability across various applications. We use **Figtree** as the primary English font, offering a modern and clean aesthetic that enhances our brand identity. For numerical content, we utilize **DIN-PRO**, providing clarity and a professional look. For multilingual support, we adopt **Noto Sans**, which ensures consistent typography across different languages. This system guarantees global readability and a cohesive visual experience across diverse media and platforms.
